• Välkommen till min visuella värld

My work uses graphic design, video, publishing, performance, makeup and digital visual worlds. While “Graphic design” holds a lot of things, I am seeing it as a starting point in my own practice to expand graphic design out of the mediums and contexts it is mostly linked to.

I am interested in cultural identity from a queer perspective, the components you can play with and steer how you present yourself and how you could be perceived, the valuation and time frame of trends, Quick Fixes and Makeover, contemporary platforms etc.
